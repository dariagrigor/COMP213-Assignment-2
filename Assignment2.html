Websites About Protected Areas Issues

One third of all land is degrading due to human activity such as forestry and food production. Such activity has a negative effect on wildlife and ecosystems. One solution is the creation of protected areas which include wilderness areas, national parks, nature reserves and conservation areas. The purpose of protected areas is to conserve nature, promote well-being and sustainable development.

IUCN (International Union for Conservation of Nature) is an international union which brings together governments with the common goal of protecting nature. IUCN works to develop strategies and programs to address the global issues of climate change.
